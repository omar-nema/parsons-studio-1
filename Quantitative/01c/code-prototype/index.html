Project Overview

This project proposes to use a data visualization approach to show a viewer how they viewed a particular piece of art. As a proof-of-concept, I am looking to collect data on how a small sample of viewers look at a single piece of artwork.

Thank you for agreeing to participate! This will take about 15 minutes total.

As a first step, we’ll need to calibrate your webcam to track your eye movement.

Calibrate

Before we get started, we need to calibrate WebGazer (the eyetracking library used here). Please attempt to first calibrate on webgazer's site for practice. And then return here, read instructions, and proceed (sorry I know it's a pain!).

If you're unable to get a calibration rate of 70% or higher on webgazer, please do not proceed with the rest of the study.
